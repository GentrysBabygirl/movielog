About

Hi there. I'm Frank. This site is a catalog of my movie-watching adventures. I keep a searchable viewing log where I track everything I see, and I try to review as much as I can. If you're curious about the star ratings, I wrote a piece on how I grade. Once upon a time, I reviewed everything I watched, but these days I aim for quality over quantity. I’m not a great writer. Maybe not even a good one. But with practice I can be better.
